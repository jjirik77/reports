Strengthening the National Cyber Talent Pipeline

The EPNC addresses a critical national priority by linking high schools directly to National Centers of Academic Excellence in Cybersecurity (NCAE-C). We empower local school systems with flexible, high-quality, and standards-based cybersecurity education.

Our Purpose

To build a sustainable and inclusive pipeline of cybersecurity talent by creating robust pathways from high school programs directly to NCAE-C designated institutions.

Our Scope

Delivering an integrated strategy blending open-source curriculum, faculty development, and a national virtual cyber range to empower educators and students nationwide.

Future Vision: Aligning with the DoD 8140/DCWF

Building a DoD-Ready Cyber Workforce: Pathways from K-12 to Federal Service

Our vision is to build a robust, scalable, and DoD-ready cyber talent pipeline. We achieve this by intentionally aligning K-12 cybersecurity education with the DoD Cyber Workforce Framework (DCWF) and creating seamless pathways for students to advance to NCAE-C institutions—the primary feeders for the federal workforce.

AI & DCWF Integration

Integrate AI competencies and map all curriculum modules to DCWF work roles for DoD 8140 focus.

Role-Focused Labs

Broaden national range access, matching every lab and challenge to specific DCWF tasks.

Pathway Recognition

Formal badging for programs demonstrating strong, articulated pathways to NCAE-C institutions.

WBL Scaling

Invest in industry onboarding to scale internship and apprenticeship occupational blends.

Sustainable Funding

Secure multi-year funding to safeguard federal investment in a future-ready workforce.

Digital Divide Bridge

Targeted programs to address infrastructure disparities for DCWF-aligned training in rural areas.

Advisor Empowerment

Enhance training for counselors to advise on DoD cyber workforce roles and certifications.

Gamified Mastery

Expand gamified modules and competitions to boost motivation for DCWF-aligned knowledge.

Modular DCWF Curric

Modular open-source curriculum allowing educators to adapt content while meeting DCWF standards.

Adaptive DoD Needs

Continuous improvement cycle to keep programs responsive to evolving DoD 8140 directives.
