Project Summary

This initiative created comprehensive systems across four interconnected areas to build sustainable cybersecurity education pathways: high school pathways, articulation agreements, concurrent enrollment, and transfer programs.

Standards-aligned curriculum for CS, IT, and Business.

Articulation Agreements

Accelerated early college credit accumulation for secondary students.
